Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

quadfixr ("the app") is an FPV drone maintenance tracking application. This policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

Data We Collect

quadfixr stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Drone names, notes, and categories
  • Component types, statuses, and maintenance history
  • Photos you choose to attach to drones or components (captured via camera or selected from your gallery)
  • Action camera details and Care Refresh contract information

How Your Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored in a local database on your device
  • Photos are stored in the app's private storage folder
  • No data is ever transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry is collected

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera — to take photos of drones and components
  • Photo library / media access — to select existing photos from your gallery

These permissions are used solely for attaching photos to your maintenance records. The app does not access your camera or photos for any other purpose.

Backup & Export

The app allows you to create a local backup file (ZIP format) containing your data and photos. This file is saved to a location you choose on your device or shared via apps you select. quadfixr does not upload or transmit backup files.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data persists until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes the local database and all associated data
  • You can export your data at any time using the backup feature before uninstalling

Children's Privacy

quadfixr does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
